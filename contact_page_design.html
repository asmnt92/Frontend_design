Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our event management system

After registering as an organizer, you can create events from your dashboard. Click "Create Event" and fill in the required details including event name, description, date, time, category, and upload an image. Once submitted, your event will be live on the platform.
User roles can only be changed by administrators. If you need to change your role from participant to organizer or vice versa, please contact our support team with your request. We'll review it and make the necessary changes.
Participants can RSVP to events with a single click. You'll receive an email confirmation immediately, and the event will appear in your personal dashboard. Our system prevents duplicate RSVPs, so each user can only register once per event.
First, check your spam folder. If you still can't find the activation email, you can request a new one from the login page by clicking "Resend Activation Email". If the problem persists, contact our support team for immediate assistance.
All your RSVP'd events are available in your Participant Dashboard. You can view upcoming events, see event details, and cancel your RSVP if needed. You'll also receive email reminders before each event.
